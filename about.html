About Me

My name is Michael Walters, an aspiring full stack developer with big dreams.
I create websites and apps with simple and intuitive UI and clean written, industry standards code and I love what I do!

I'm driven by my passion for technology and I love seeing mine or a clients ideas come to life. My passion for technology started at a young age when I helped my Father assemble PC's and learnt about his time in university studying computer science and the rest is history!

This drive to be part of the ever evolving world of technology has since been ramped up a gear during my time as a system analyst for Fourth Hospitality.
This role has pieced together my understanding of how a technology company functions and given me a detailed overview of all stages of the development cycle.

I believe that a workplace / employees function most efficiently when they're happy. Which is why I employ mindfullness daily to reduce the stress of everyday life and keep myself on top form mentally and physically.
